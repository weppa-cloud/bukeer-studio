## Font Allowlist (30 fonts from theme-sdk)

**Serif** (headings): Playfair Display, DM Serif Display, Merriweather, Cormorant Garamond, Lora, Crimson Text, Libre Baskerville, Source Serif 4

**Sans-Serif** (headings or body): Inter, Montserrat, Nunito, DM Sans, Outfit, Lato, Source Sans 3, Poppins, Raleway, Open Sans, Roboto, Work Sans, Manrope, Plus Jakarta Sans

**Display** (hero only): Space Grotesk, Sora, Figtree, Urbanist, Lexend, Albert Sans

**Pairing Rules:**
- Serif heading + Sans body = elegance (luxury, boutique, romantic, cultural)
- Sans heading + Sans body = modern clarity (adventure, corporate, eco, tropical)
- Same family = unified (use weight contrast)
- NEVER two serif fonts together
- NEVER more than 2 families total
