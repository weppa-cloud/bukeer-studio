## Recommended Formats

1. Keep Search exact-first as primary format for BR/MX/ES/CL until CRM quality is proven.
2. Improve RSAs to Good/Excellent before scaling. Use up to 15 headlines and 4 descriptions, exact intent, city-origin proof, full-package promise and no-flight/no-hotel-only qualifiers.
3. Add campaign-specific assets before budget increases: 6+ sitelinks, 4+ images, 6-8 callouts, 2-3 structured snippets, and call asset only if calls are operationally useful.
4. Test lead form assets only with qualifying fields and CRM attribution; otherwise they can increase low-quality conversions.
5. Hold Performance Max/Demand Gen until offline conversion quality is stable and there is enough creative/audience data.
