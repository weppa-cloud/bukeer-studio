## Category 1 — Indexability + brand sovereignty

### #C1-P0-01 — Brand cannibalization fix (home ES + EN)
- **Severity:** P0 (revenue-protect)
- **URLs:** `/`, `https://en.colombiatours.travel/`
- **Issue:** Query `colombia tours travel` ranks pos 3.85 (47 clicks/175 impr/26.86% CTR). Brand should rank #1. EN subdomain root pos 38.29.
- **Fix:**
  1. ES home `<title>` → `ColombiaTours.Travel — Tours y Paquetes 2026 con Planner Experto`.
  2. EN home `<title>` → `ColombiaTours.Travel — Tours & Travel Packages 2026 by Local Planners`.
  3. Add `WebSite.alternateName: ["Colombia Tours Travel","ColombiaTours"]` to JSON-LD.
  4. Force `og:site_name` consistent on both subdomains.
- **Hypothesis:** Brand pos 3.85 → 1.0 in 21d; brand CTR 26.86% → 35-45%.
- **Baseline:** GSC 28d 2026-03-27→2026-04-24.
- **Owner:** TBD (Studio Rol 1 — title is template-driven, lives in `app/site/[subdomain]/page.tsx` metadata).
- **Eval:** 2026-05-18.

### #C1-P0-02 — EN subdomain canibalization audit
- **Severity:** P0
- **URLs:** EN subdomain blog set (top: `/tipos-de-mamas/`, `/los-10-mejores-destinos-para-conocer-colombia/` — 10,270 impressions/CTR 0.19%).
- **Issue:** EN subdomain serves ES content with weak hreflang reciprocity, splitting ranking equity.
- **Fix:**
  1. Crawl all EN URLs vs ES root counterparts; produce hreflang reciprocity table.
  2. For ES content on EN subdomain, demote to canonical ES URL or remove from EN sitemap.
  3. Add `<link rel="alternate" hreflang="x-default">` consistent across pairs.
- **Hypothesis:** Recovered impressions accrue to ES URL → +20-30% organic clicks on consolidated set in 45d.
- **Baseline:** EN subdomain captured 107 clicks / 26,649 impressions / CTR 0.40% (sum top GSC).
- **Owner:** TBD (joint Rol 1 + Rol 2 data audit).
- **Eval:** 2026-06-08.

### #C1-P1-03 — Sitemap normalization (`x-default` hreflang)
- **Severity:** P1
- **URLs:** All 709 sitemap URLs.
- **Issue:** Sitemap uses normalized URLs without trailing slash; reciprocity sampled OK, but `x-default` validation pending.
