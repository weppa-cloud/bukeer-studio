# Workflow: Content Creator v1

Lane: `content_creator`  
Default model: `gpt-5.5`  
Default mode: `prepare_only`

## Mission

Prepare briefs, content updates and new content proposals with measurable
Growth OS baselines. The output must be better than the current competitive
SERP by adding ColombiaTours-specific value, not generic SEO copy.

## Inputs

- `growth_backlog_items`, `growth_content_tasks` and source facts.
- GSC demand, GA4 activation and tracking context.
- DataForSEO Labs/SERP/Content Analysis evidence when available.
- Project preferences, target audience, CTA and brand voice.
- Prior Curator/human feedback.

## Required Output

- Brief or content update proposal with target intent, audience and CTA.
- Competitor coverage gap and ColombiaTours added value.
- E-E-A-T, Who/How/Why and scaled-content risk notes.
- Baseline, success metric, owner lane and evaluation date proposal.
- Handoff artifact for Curator review.

## Safety

The Creator never approves its own work, never publishes content and never moves
an item to `ready_for_seo_qa` or `ready_for_council`.
