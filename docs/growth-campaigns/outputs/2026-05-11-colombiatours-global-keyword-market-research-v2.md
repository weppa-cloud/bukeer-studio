# ColombiaTours Global Keyword, Market, And Product Research V2

Date: 2026-05-11
Scope: global paid-search and product demand investigation, not limited to current campaigns.
Account evidence: Google Ads customer `1261189646` / ColombiaTours.
Primary KPI: qualified CRM opportunity, not raw click volume or legacy platform conversion count.

## Executive Readout

The global opportunity is broader than the current Mexico, Spain, Chile, and USA tests. ColombiaTours should not activate every country at once, but the research identifies several non-current markets with enough demand to justify landing creation and controlled tests.

The strongest global expansion candidates are:

1. **Brazil**: high commercial package demand, low CPC, Portuguese landing required before Ads.
2. **France**: strong premium intent for `voyage organisé colombie` and `voyage sur mesure colombie`; high-value but requires French landing and sales handling.
3. **Germany**: good `rundreise` demand and premium tour intent; requires German landing and careful travel-planner positioning.
4. **Argentina**: Spanish-speaking, low CPC, solid `paquetes a colombia` volume; quality and purchasing-power risk must be controlled.
5. **Italy**: smaller than France/Germany but clear `viaggio organizzato colombia` demand.
6. **Uruguay**: small market, but unusually strong commercial phrase `paquetes a colombia desde uruguay`.

Current campaign markets still matter:

- **Mexico** remains the most scalable Spanish acquisition engine.
- **Spain** is the best current quality signal after recent changes.
- **USA** has premium upside, but should run only exact/phrase terms with EN landing and strict negatives.
- **Chile** remains a low-budget validation market, not a scale engine alone.

Global does not mean broad match or global targeting. It means building a ranked market/product matrix, creating language-specific landing pages where needed, and opening tightly controlled tests only when tracking and sales capacity are ready.

## Activation Plan

### A. Keep Optimizing Current Revenue Tests

- Mexico: keep active; do not open broad match. Expand exact/phrase only around package and organized-trip terms that already show signal.
- Spain: keep active; segment premium/multidestino, Cartagena, and Eje Cafetero. Do not scale until CRM confirms quality.
- Chile: keep low-budget; focus San Andres and Colombia packages, with strict cheap/flight/hotel negatives.
- USA: reopen only if exact-only and EN landing QA passes; budget cap should stay low until first qualified lead.

### B. Build Global Landing Backlog Before Spending

Create these landing pages in priority order:

1. `/pt/pacotes-colombia` or `/viagem-colombia-pacotes` for Brazil.
2. `/fr/voyage-colombie-sur-mesure` for France.
3. `/de/kolumbien-rundreise` for Germany.
4. `/viajes-a-colombia-desde-argentina` for Argentina.
5. `/it/viaggio-organizzato-colombia` for Italy.
6. `/viajes-a-colombia-desde-uruguay` for Uruguay.
7. `/santander-san-gil-barichara` as global SEO/product gap.

Each page must have:

- market-specific H1 and copy;
- package/product proof, not generic destination copy;
- visible WhatsApp/WAFlow CTA;
- preserved `gclid`, UTMs, `_fbp`, `_fbc`, and `reference_code`;
- events `whatsapp_cta_click`, `waflow_submit`, and CRM linkage;
- noindex only if intentionally not ready for organic indexation.

## Global Negatives And Risk Controls

Apply these globally, with local-language variants per campaign:

- Flights and airlines: `vuelos`, `tiquetes`, `aerolineas`, `flight`, `airline`, `vols`, `flug`, `flugticket`, `voo`, `passagem`.
- Accommodation-only: `hotel`, `hostel`, `airbnb`, `booking`, `trivago`, `hôtel`, `hotel barato`.
- Low-budget: `barato`, `gratis`, `2x1`, `ofertas`, `low cost`, `mochilero`, `pas cher`, `günstig`.
- Immigration/admin: `visa`, `pasaporte`, `requisitos`, `migracion`, `emploi`, `trabajo`, `work`.
- Information-only: `mapa`, `clima`, `weather`, `dangerous`, `safe`, `population`, `currency`.
- Local day tours: `free walking tour`, `day trip`, `guatape day`, `jardin day`, `city tour medellin`.
- Misspellings/irrelevant: `columbia` where it indicates university/clothing/context not Colombia travel.

## Decision Rules

- No broad match in global expansion.
- No paid launch without market-specific landing and event tracking.
- No budget scale without CRM stage evidence, not just Google Ads conversions.
- Pause any experiment after COP 120k spend without qualified conversation, unless seller manually flags high intent.
- Scale 20-30% only after 3 days with clean search terms, preserved attribution, and seller-confirmed lead quality.
- Markets with language mismatch must run SEO/content first or paid only with very low budget.
